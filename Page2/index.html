    Taronga is a Cashless Zoo:

    Taronga’s Zoos are now cashless, with contactless payments accepted when purchasing General Admission Zoo tickets and across our retail, food and beverage outlets.

    Please book your tickets online in advance or pay by card at the admissions desk upon your arrival. Find out more, including our cashless kiosk here.
