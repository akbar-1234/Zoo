Taronga is a Cashless Zoo

Both our Dubbo and Sydney Zoos are now cashless, with contactless payments accepted when purchasing General Admission Zoo tickets and across our retail and food and beverage outlets. Please book your tickets online in advance or pay by card at the admissions desk upon your arrival.ï¿½ 

Gift vouchers

We have gift vouchers available for both our Dubbo and Sydney Zoos. A Taronga Zoo gift voucher is the perfect gift for your family, friends, colleagues or employees. Vouchers are for general admission to Taronga Zoo Sydney or Taronga Western Plains Zoo Dubbo on any day of the year and are valid for three years from date of purchase.
